City Traverse — Temperature Analysis

This tool reads a combined CSV file from the temporary_data/ folder, applies temperature corrections, and produces interactive outputs: a distance-vs-temperature plot with a Vapor Pressure Deficit (VPD) overlay, and a Folium map with color-coded temperature markers. Clicking a point on the distance plot pans the map to that location.
