Project 2 — City Traverse

City Traverse analyzes temperature data collected along urban driving routes to study the Urban Heat Island effect. The pipeline reads a combined CSV of sensor readings, applies corrections, and produces three interactive outputs: a scatter plot of corrected temperature vs. local time, a distance-vs-temperature plot with vapor pressure deficit (VPD) overlay, and a Folium map with color-coded temperature markers that link back to the plots. Clicking a point on any chart highlights the corresponding location on the map.

The script supports two distance modes — distance from the starting point or cumulative travel distance — and can optionally restrict the x-axis to the first half of the traverse for easier comparison of the outbound and return legs.
